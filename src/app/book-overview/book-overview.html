Our BookNest

Welcome to Our BookNest —a cozy digital home for every book in our house. Each room on the map above represents a real space in our home, and every bookshelf has been carefully numbered so you can always find exactly where a book lives. To locate a book, simply look up its room and shelf number. The shelves are counted clockwise, beginning from the front of the house—our “street side”—as shown on the map. With this simple system, exploring our collection becomes a gentle wander through our personal library. Enjoy your time in Our BookNest!
